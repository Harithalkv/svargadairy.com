Fresh milk

Our milk is the purest and unaltered type of organic milk. Produced by cows that have been reared with care and reverence. These cows are fed with fodder, greens, vegetables, jaggery and natural foods, clean water and kept in healthy conditions.

Our farm fresh A2 milk is easy to digest for your little one’s tummy, as the A2 protein is similar to that found in breast milk. It is selectively sourced from cows who are fed a plant-based diet and are never treated with antibiotics or artificial growth hormones. It is rich in protein, which is an important building block in everything from muscle tissue to skin and blood.

A2 milk from our farm help to keep your blood pressure healthy. It helps your mood and strengthens your immunity. No special solutions or medicines are given to them to alter their milk. No machines are used and the upkeep and milking process is traditional and in the vedic way.
